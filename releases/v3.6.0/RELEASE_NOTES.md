# NoteKar 3.6.0

NoteKar 3.6.0 is a focused refinement release centered on faster compact history, cleaner access to quick actions, and the earlier polish work carried forward into a tighter daily-use build.

## What's New

- Updated the app to `3.6.0+8`.
- Restored compact History to the denser 3.0.0-style cards so faster scanning feels closer to the earlier build.
- Kept History as a simple Normal / Compact switch instead of adding more density modes.
- Moved Quick Actions out of Privacy and into Accessibility so launcher shortcuts sit with interaction controls.
- Kept the calmer Action Color dots, Haptic Style, Backup Reminder, Data Health, and Privacy Lock improvements.

## APK Files

- `notekar-3.6.0-universal.apk` works on all supported Android ABIs.
- `notekar-3.6.0-arm64-v8a.apk` is recommended for most modern Android phones.
- `notekar-3.6.0-armeabi-v7a.apk` is for older 32-bit ARM devices.
- `notekar-3.6.0-x86_64.apk` is for x86_64 devices and emulators.

SHA-256 checksums are available in `SHA256SUMS.txt`.
